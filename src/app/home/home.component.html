Varieties of CDR's

Voice Call detail Record

Data record that contains information about a phone call, including call start and end times, phone numbers, and call duration, used for billing and analysis in telecom networks.

Sms/Mms Call detail Record

Data record containing information about text and multimedia messages, including sender and receiver, timestamps, and message content, used for billing and analysis in telecom networks.

Voip Call detail Record

Data record that logs details of voice over IP (VoIP) calls, including call initiation, endpoints, duration, and other relevant call information, used for billing and analysis in VoIP networks.

Location Call detail Record

Data record that captures information about a mobile device's location, including timestamps, coordinates, and cell tower data, used for tracking and billing in mobile networks.

Datauseage Call detail Record

Data record that logs data consumption, including timestamps, volume, and data type, used for billing and analysis in mobile and internet service providers.
